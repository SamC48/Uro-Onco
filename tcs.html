Question Answer
Congress dates and venue? Dates: 1 - 3 August 2025

Venue: The Capital, on the Park
Website
What are the congress hours? Friday: 16:00 - 18:00
Saturday: 08:00 - 17:00
Sunday: 09:00 - 10:30
The above times do not include the social functions on Friday and Saturday
Who can attend the congress? We invite medical professionals from all disciplines to attend
Is there a virtual attendance option? Unfortunately, no
What is the congress theme for this year? Uro-Oncology and collaboration within medicine
How do I register for the congress? Registration will open mid-February 2025
Once registration has opened, one may use the "register" links throughout the website to register online
What are the registration fees? R2 500.00 per person
Are group discounts available? Unfortunately, no
What does the registration fee include? Access to the entire congress and social events
Refreshments and lunch
Where can I find the congress program? Here (Provisional)
Are there Continuing Professional Development (CPD) points offered? Yes
Will there be an exhibition area? Yes
How do I become a sponsor or exhibitor? Please contact the project manager, Juanita Males
Are there any networking opportunities? Yes
Are meals provided during the congress? Yes, lunch is provided on Saturday
Is the congress venue accessible for people with disabilities? Yes
